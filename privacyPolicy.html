Privacy Policy for shubhearn

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our fintech services and related work. We are committed to protecting your privacy and using your personal information only in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

What information do we collect?

We collect personal information from you when you create an account, use our services, or contact us for support. This information may include:

Your name, email address, and phone number
Your date of birth and Social Security number (for certain services)
Your financial account information (such as bank account numbers and routing numbers)
Your transaction history
Your IP address and device information
 

How do we use your information?

We use your personal information to provide you with our services, to improve our services, and to communicate with you. We may also use your personal information to detect and prevent fraud, to comply with legal requirements, and to enforce our terms of service.

How do we share your information?

We may share your personal information with third-party service providers who help us provide our services. For example, we may share your information with payment processors, fraud prevention services, and customer support providers. We may also share your information with third parties for marketing purposes, but only with your consent.

How do we protect your information?

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We use a variety of security measures, including encryption, firewalls, and access controls.

Your Choices

You have the following choices with respect to your personal information:

You can choose not to provide us with personal information. However, this may limit your ability to use our services.
You can choose to opt out of marketing communications from us. To do so, please contact us at [email protected]
You can access, correct, or delete your personal information. To do so, please contact us at [email protected]
 

Changes to this Privacy Policy

Related work for fintech services
